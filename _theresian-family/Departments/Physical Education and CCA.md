<h2>"Move to Inspire, Inspire to Move"</h2>
<p><strong>Mission<br></strong>To provide a comprehensive 4 to 5-year long
Physical Education Programme to develop the physical, intellectual, social
and emotional aspects of all our students through physical activities.</p>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>
<br>To be a vibrant Physical Education department that empowers students with
the knowledge, skills, and values to live active, healthy, and balanced
lives. Through engaging, pupil-centred experiences, we cultivate self-confidence,
creativity, leadership, teamwork, and a passion for lifelong fitness and
well-being.</p>
<p><strong>Key Programmes</strong>
<br>
</p>
<p><u>Core Physical Education Curriculum</u>
</p>
<p>A progressive, pupil-centred PE programme designed to develop fundamental
movement skills, game sense, and fitness literacy.</p>
<p>Focus areas include:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Games &amp; Sports Skills – e.g. Netball, Badminton, Floorball, Volleyball,
Hockey, Tack &amp; Field.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fitness &amp; Health – Aerobic conditioning, circuit training, and personal
fitness goal setting.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Outdoor Education – Orienteering, basic navigation, and environmental
appreciation.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><u> Inter-Class and Inter-House Games</u>
</p>
<p>Annual competitions that foster teamwork, sportsmanship, and school spirit.
<br>Opportunities for every student to participate, compete, and lead.
<br>Highlights collaboration, perseverance, and fair play — values core to
the Theresian spirit.</p>
<p><u>Education through Adventure &amp; Outdoor Learning</u>
</p>
<p>Camp-based and outdoor education programmes to build resilience, self-confidence,
and cooperation.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Secondary 1 EFL Camp &amp; 2 OAL Camps.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Seondary 4 Dragonboating Programme.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sec 1 Safe Cycling and Mini-expedition.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Orienteering and low-element challenges.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Environmental awareness and sustainability activities.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><u>Health and Safety Education</u>
</p>
<p>Regular reinforcement of safety practices, injury prevention, and responsible
participation.
<br>Embedding first aid awareness and safe use of equipment in lessons.</p>
<p><u>Leadership through Sports Programme</u>
</p>
<p>Developing leadership, responsibility, and peer motivation through sport-related
roles.
<br>Opportunities include: LLP Champions, &amp; Sports Leaders training.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Leading warm-ups, mini-games, and class lessons/ challenges.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Supporting inter-class and inter-house competitions.</p>
</li>
</ul>
